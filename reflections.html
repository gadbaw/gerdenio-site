On Suffering, Presence, and Grace

A conversation with Gerdenio “Sonny” Manuel, S.J., Ph.D.

Psychologist, teacher, and Jesuit priest, Sonny Manuel has spent a lifetime accompanying people through pain. He reflects on presence, the question beneath all others — “Do you love me?” — and a God who does not trade in outcomes.
