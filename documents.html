Documents

Patient forms.

When your care involves coordinating with another provider, we'll ask you to authorize the exchange of records. Current clients can complete and sign these forms securely online — we'll send you a private link, with nothing to print, scan, or mail. You're welcome to review the blank forms here first.

01 — Releasing records

Authorization to release records to another party

Authorizes us to share confidential information about your treatment with a provider or party you designate — for example, your physician, psychiatrist, or another therapist coordinating your care.

Current clients can sign securely online; we'll send a private link. Takes about two minutes.

02 — Requesting records

Authorization to release records to this practice

Authorizes another provider to share confidential information about your treatment with us — useful when you'd like a prior therapist, physician, or hospital to send records into your care here.

Current clients can sign securely online; we'll send a private link. Takes about two minutes.

A note on privacy. These forms are signed through our own secure, HIPAA-aligned system — we send each client a private, time-limited link rather than collecting health information on this public page. Please don't include sensitive clinical details in the contact form; if you have questions, write to us at hello@gerdenio.com or call (415) 569-3735 and we'll help you directly.
